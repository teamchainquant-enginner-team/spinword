# Social-casino sandbox boundaries

This repository is a technical sandbox, not legal advice or a claim of licensing, certification, or regulatory compliance.

- Loot Coins are free, have no cash or crypto value, and cannot be bought, transferred, withdrawn, redeemed, or converted.
- Spin Coins are purchased virtual credits with no cash or crypto value. They cannot be transferred, withdrawn, redeemed, resold, or converted.
- Crypto purchases are disabled until an approved provider, credentials, server feature flags, signed webhooks, confirmation tracking, reconciliation, under/overpayment operations, and applicable legal review are supplied.
- No private-key custody exists in the frontend.
- No real-money redemption or wagering path exists.
- No autoplay, loss-chasing promotions, automatic play increases, fake winners, fake activity, or individualized odds are implemented.
- Total-return multipliers include the original play amount. A return below `1x` is a net loss and is displayed as such.
- All players on the same published version use the same paytable and word pool.

Before any production launch, obtain qualified legal review for every target market and complete identity, age/region, consumer-protection, privacy, payments, tax, sanctions, responsible-play, security, accessibility, incident-response, and data-retention work appropriate to that launch.
